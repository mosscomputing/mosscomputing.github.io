Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

MOSS is a local-first cryptographic signing library. We built it with privacy as a core principle.

What MOSS Does Not Collect

The MOSS SDK runs entirely on your infrastructure. It does not:

  • Send data to our servers
  • Collect usage analytics
  • Store your keys or signatures
  • Phone home for any reason

Your keys stay on your machine. Your signatures stay in your system. We never see them.

Website

This website (mosscomputing.com) is a static site. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services. We do not collect personal information through this website.

Discord

If you join our Discord community, Discord's privacy policy applies to your participation there. We do not collect or store Discord user data outside the platform.

Email

If you contact us at moss@iampass.com, we retain your email only as needed to respond to your inquiry.

Changes

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. But our commitment to local-first, privacy-respecting software won't change.
